Metroid (Unity Remaster)

We used unity, a cross-platform game engine used to develop 2D and 3D video games, to make our own "Metroid". ChatGPT was an excellent resource for our team to gain familiarity with Unity.

I playtested the original NES "Metroid" and provided suggestions on the desired gameplay outcomes for our "Metroid" game, such as state transitions and the conditions for Samus's animation.

I re-implemented the enemies in "Metroid" and their interactions with Samus, including Samus's damage aesthetics and the unique behaviors of enemies when damaged.

I created the majority of the animations in "Metroid" and some sound effects utilizing the sound system designed by my teammate.

I focused on specific map tiles to increase the realism of the game, such as lava, vertical/horizontal pipes, grass fields, etc.

I designed multiple interfaces, including the static class Utils.

I designed the final room of the customized level.
